## Dashboard overview

The dashboard is your home screen in eggz. It gives you a snapshot of your business finances and quick access to upload new documents.

## Analytics widgets

The top of the dashboard shows four summary widgets for the current month:

| Widget | What it shows | Data source |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Monthly Spend** | Total outbound expenses | Receipts + Bills from Xero |
| **Revenue** | Total inbound income | Invoices from Xero |
| **Quote Pipeline** | Value of open quotes | Quotes from Xero |
| **Expense Breakdown** | Spending by category | Categorised receipt line items |

Click any widget to drill down into the detailed analytics view with filters by month, source type, and category.

<Tip>Widgets update automatically as new data syncs from Xero or as you upload new receipts.</Tip>

## Upload area

Below the widgets, a drag-and-drop zone lets you quickly upload receipts. Drop a PDF or image file to start processing immediately. See [Uploading Documents](/guides/uploading-documents) for details.

## Recent activity

The dashboard shows a notification inbox with your most recent activity -- processed receipts, sync updates, and other events.

## Receipt table

Below the activity feed, a table shows your recent receipts with status, merchant, amount, and date. Click any row to open the receipt detail view.

## Navigation

### Desktop

The desktop header contains:

| Element | Location | Purpose |
|---------|----------|---------|
| **eggz logo** | Top left | Click to return to the dashboard |
| **Main menu** | Top centre | Home, Sales, Expenses, Quotes, Customers |
| **Search** | Top right area | Search across your documents |
| **Dark mode toggle** | Top right | Switch between light and dark themes |
| **Settings** | Top right icon | General, Billing, Users, Integrations |
| **Notifications** | Top right bell | View recent activity and updates |
| **Profile** | Top right avatar | My Profile, Sign Out |

### Main menu items

| Menu Item | Page | What you will find |
|-----------|------|--------------------|
| **Home** | `/` | Dashboard with analytics, upload area, recent receipts |
| **Sales** | `/invoices` | All sales invoices synced from Xero |
| **Expenses** | `/expenses` | Receipts and bills in tabbed view |
| **Quotes** | `/quotes` | All quotes synced from Xero |
| **Customers** | `/accounts` | Customer accounts synced from Xero |

### Mobile

On mobile devices, eggz uses a simplified layout:

- **Top bar**: Logo, settings, notifications, profile
- **Bottom dock**: Home, Camera (quick capture), Expenses

<Tip>The camera button on the mobile bottom dock opens your device camera to photograph a receipt. The photo is automatically uploaded and processed.</Tip>

### Settings tabs

Access settings from the gear icon in the header. Settings are organised into four tabs:

| Tab | What you can configure |
|-----|------------------------|
| **General** | Workspace name, timezone, reporting currency, industry |
| **Billing** | View and manage your subscription plan |
| **Users** | Invite and manage team members |
| **Integrations** | Connect or disconnect Xero and Gmail |
