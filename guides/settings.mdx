## Accessing settings

Click the gear icon in the top-right corner of the header to open Settings. Settings are organised into four tabs.

## General settings

Configure your workspace basics:

| Setting | What it controls |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Workspace name** | Your company or team name displayed throughout eggz |
| **Timezone** | Affects date display and reporting periods |
| **Reporting currency** | Default currency for analytics and dashboards |
| **Industry** | Helps AI categorise expenses more accurately |

## Billing

View and manage your subscription:

- See your current plan and usage
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- View document processing limits and current usage
- Manage payment methods

### Plan limits

| Feature | What it limits |
|---------|---------------|
| **Document processing** | Number of receipts you can upload and process per month |
| **Team members** | Number of users in your workspace |
| **Integrations** | Available third-party connections |

## Users

Manage your team:

- Invite new members to your workspace by email
- View current team members and their roles
- Remove members who no longer need access

<Note>User management is powered by WorkOS. Invited users receive an email to create their account or sign in with an existing one.</Note>

### Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|------------|
| **Admin** | Full access to all data, settings, integrations, and user management |
| **Member** | Access to own data only. Cannot manage settings, billing, or users. |

## Integrations

Connect and manage external services:

### Xero

- **Connect/disconnect** your Xero account
- **Sync data** manually: Customers, Chart of Accounts, Invoices, Bills, Quotes
- **Map accounts**: Assign eggz expense categories to Xero account codes

See [Xero Integration](/guides/xero-sync) for the full setup guide.

### Gmail

- **Connect/disconnect** your Gmail account using WorkOS Pipes
- When connected, eggz can detect invoices, bills, and receipts in your inbox

<Tip>Both integrations can be set up during onboarding or at any time from this settings page.</Tip>
