<Update label="2026-07-09--dashboard-save-cancel-and-personal-views" description="Web App — Customize mode gets a real Save/Cancel, a personal default reporting view, and clearer expense drill-downs" tags={["Web App"]}>

**9 July 2026** · **Web App**

- **Customize mode now has Save and Cancel.** Nothing changes until you click **Save** — **Cancel**, or **Esc**, discards your edits, and a failed save keeps you in edit mode so nothing's lost. **Reset to default** only takes effect once you save it. New dashboards open with **Revenue, Monthly Spend, Expense Breakdown, Quote Pipeline**, left to right. See [Personalising your dashboard](/help/guides/personalising-dashboard).
- **Widgets resize in clean steps** — quarter, third, half, or full width, one or two units tall — so your layout always tiles neatly. While customizing, widgets are for dragging and resizing only; charts and links inside them aren't clickable until you save or cancel.
- **Pin your own default reporting view.** Click the pin next to the **Billed / Collected / Outstanding** toggle to make your current view your personal default — click it again to follow your organisation's default instead. It only changes what you see. See [Two things worth knowing](/help/guides/reporting-views#two-things-worth-knowing).
- **Clearer Expense Breakdown drill-downs.** Clicking a slice shows the records behind the number in your active view — including whole documents with **no line detail** — and the rows always add up to the card. See [Seeing what's behind a number](/help/guides/reporting-views#seeing-whats-behind-a-number).
- **Simpler chart taps on mobile.** Tapping a chart now shows a tooltip with a **Show More** link to the full report, instead of navigating straight there.

</Update>

**8 July 2026** · **Web App**

See [Drill into the numbers](/help/guides/dashboard#drill-into-the-numbers).

<Update label="2026-06-16--consent-reading-dashboard-pinned" description="Extension v1.3.0 — the AI asks before reading a page, a slick new dashboard sidebar with drag-and-drop, and pinned workspaces" tags={["Chrome Extension"]}>

**16 June 2026** · **Chrome Extension** · **v1.3.0**

- **The AI asks before it reads a page.** The chat sees your tabs' **names and addresses** by default — but to read **what's actually on a page** (or one of your records), it now pops up a permission card: **Allow once**, **Always allow** (for this conversation), or **Deny**. Nothing on a page leaves your browser until you say yes. See [AI assistant & search](/help/unscrambled-tabs/ai-and-search#it-asks-before-it-reads-a-page).
- **Cleaner reading.** When you do approve, the extension pulls out the real article or content and skips menus, ads, and clutter — so the AI works from the good stuff, not the junk.
- **A slick new dashboard sidebar.** The dashboard now has a slim **icon rail**: hover a folder to fan out its workspaces, pin the full list open with **⌘\\ / Ctrl+\\**, and grab quick **Switch** / **Open in new window** actions on hover. Plus **drag-and-drop** to reorder folders, workspaces, and tabs (or drop a tab onto a workspace to move it). See [The dashboard sidebar](/help/unscrambled-tabs/workspaces#the-dashboard-sidebar).
- **Pinned workspaces.** Pin the workspaces you live in so they're always one click away at the top of the rail. See [Pinned workspaces](/help/unscrambled-tabs/workspaces#pinned-workspaces).
- **Capture the original bill.** "Save to eggz" now sends the **actual PDF** — filename and all pages intact — instead of a screenshot, so multi-page invoices arrive complete. See [Capture bills from any page](/help/unscrambled-tabs/capture-bills).
- **A warmer welcome.** First-run setup now helps you **create your first workspace** from the tabs you already have open (and pins it) — no more empty screen.
- **Easier on the eyes.** Sharper text contrast and readable notice banners in both **light** and **dark** mode.

</Update>

<Update label="2026-06-15--unified-ai-and-open-tabs" description="Extension v1.2.0 — one smart AI chat (no more mode switch), AI that sees every window's tabs and your workspaces, and a new Open Tabs view" tags={["Chrome Extension"]}>

**15 June 2026** · **Chrome Extension** · **v1.2.0**

- **One chat, no switch.** The two separate AI helpers (and the little toggle between them) are gone. There's now a **single chat** that quietly picks the right helper: signed in with credits → the included eggz assistant; out of credits with your own key → your key as a **backup**; signed out with a key → your key. See [AI assistant & search](/help/unscrambled-tabs/ai-and-search).
- **Your AI sees the whole browser.** The chat now knows about **every open tab in every window** (even sleeping/suspended ones, by title and address) *and* your **saved workspaces, folders, and links** — so one chat answers both your books and your tabs. Sleeping tabs stay asleep; it only reads their title and address.
- **New Open Tabs view.** Turn it on in **Settings** to see every tab across every window in one list, then **save the keepers** into a workspace. Choose the layout, sort order, and grouping. See [Open Tabs view](/help/unscrambled-tabs/open-tabs).
- **Organise, everywhere.** **Organise** moved into the chat header and now tidies tabs across **all** windows, saving groups into new or existing workspaces.
- **Out of credits? You're not stuck.** A dismissible banner offers a one-click **Upgrade**, and if you have your own key the chat keeps going on it. No key? The upgrade card also lets you **invite a friend for free credits**.
- Plus a friendlier emoji picker for workspaces/folders and tidier in-app confirmation dialogs.

</Update>

<Update label="2026-05-09--help-url-migration" description="Help Centre now lives at eggz.ai/help · 301 from help.eggz.ai" tags={["Help Docs", "Chrome Extension"]}>

**9 May 2026** · **Help Docs** · **Chrome Extension**

- The Help Centre is now at **`eggz.ai/help`** (not **`help.eggz.ai`**). Old URLs are **301-redirected** path-for-path, e.g. `help.eggz.ai/unscrambled-tabs/install` → `eggz.ai/help/unscrambled-tabs/install`.
- **Why:** keep SEO and brand on the **`eggz.ai`** domain; one host for marketing, app, and docs.
- **How:** Mintlify hosts the docs; a **Cloudflare Worker** proxies **`/help/*`** to **`eggz.mintlify.app`**. Another Worker **301**s the legacy **`help.eggz.ai`** hostname to **`eggz.ai/help/*`**. Internal links and this changelog use **`/help/...`** paths throughout.
- **Chrome Extension:** **Performance & RAM → Learn more** opens [`/help/unscrambled-tabs/performance-and-ram#performance-modes-explained`](/help/unscrambled-tabs/performance-and-ram#performance-modes-explained).

</Update>

<Update label="ext-v1-1-2" description="Chrome extension v1.1.2 · RAM modes, discard rules, multi-window binding" tags={["Chrome Extension"]}>

**Earlier (before 9 May 2026)** · **Chrome Extension**

- **Performance modes:** **Manual** (default), **Balanced**, or **Aggressive** — controls eligible inactive-tab discard via [`tabs.discard`](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/extensions/reference/api/tabs). **Free RAM now** discards safe inactive tabs in one shot; **multi-window** sweeps include all normal windows.
- **Workspace binding** per Chrome window so the side panel and dashboard follow the workspace for **that** window.
- **Safety:** never auto-discards active, pinned, audible, already discarded, `chrome://` / extension pages, non-http(s) URLs, or **Protected domains**.
- **`windows`** permission for reliable [`chrome.windows.getCurrent`](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/extensions/reference/api/windows) when routing and bindings.
- Internal RAM/performance QA checklist (aggregate metrics only); more tests for **discard eligibility**.

</Update>
