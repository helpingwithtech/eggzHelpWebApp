## What you can do

eggz works as an **app inside your AI assistant**. Drop a receipt or bill into ChatGPT or Claude and eggz reads it, **auto-categorises** every line, and shows you an editable card — then saves it straight into your eggz account. No switching tabs, no manual data entry.

<Note>You can try it **without an eggz account**. Extraction runs as a guest preview; your bills are only saved once you sign in.</Note>

## Connect eggz to your assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your assistant's connectors or apps">
    In **ChatGPT**, open **Settings → Apps & Connectors**. In **Claude**, open **Settings → Connectors**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add eggz">
    Choose **eggz** from the directory, or add it by its connector URL if you're an early-access user. (eggz is rolling out to the ChatGPT app and Claude connector directories.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're ready">
    No sign-in is needed to start — you're only asked to sign in when you save your first bill.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Capture a bill

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your document">
    Ask eggz to capture a bill and attach the file(s) — **PDF or image** (PNG, JPEG, WebP). You can send **several at once**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="eggz extracts and categorises">
    eggz reads the supplier, dates, line items, amounts and tax, and assigns an **expense category** to each line automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and edit the card">
    An interactive card appears. Edit the supplier, dates, descriptions and amounts, change any **category**, expand a bill to review its lines, or remove one. Totals update as you go. Only a supplier, a currency and at least one line item are required — everything else is optional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save to eggz">
    Select **Save to eggz**. If you're not signed in yet, your assistant prompts you to connect eggz — sign in (or sign up in one tap) and it saves automatically. Your bills appear in **[Expenses](/help/guides/expenses)**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Capturing several invoices? Upload them together and review them as a batch — each bill keeps its own categories and totals.</Tip>

## What eggz extracts

| Field | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Supplier | Name (and tax/ABN if shown on the document) |
| Dates | Issue date and due date |
| Line items | Description, quantity, unit price, and line total |
| Category | Auto-assigned per line; fully editable |
| Totals | Subtotal, tax/GST, and total — recalculated as you edit |

<Warning>AI extraction isn't perfect — always review the card before saving, especially totals on faded or photographed receipts.</Warning>

## Where your bills go

Saved bills land in **[Expenses](/help/guides/expenses)** in the eggz web app, ready to publish to **[Xero](/help/guides/xero-sync)** like any other document. The categories you choose map to your Xero accounts when you reconcile.

<Note>
Uploads from the **Unscrambled Tabs** extension enter this same capture pipeline and consume the same **document processing credits** as dashboard uploads — see [Connect your eggz account](/help/unscrambled-tabs/eggz-account).
</Note>

## Privacy

<Note>Documents you share are used only to extract your bill. Nothing is saved to an eggz account until you sign in and choose **Save to eggz**.</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which assistants are supported?">
    ChatGPT and Claude (any host that supports the Model Context Protocol apps/connectors standard). eggz is rolling out to their app and connector directories; an in-assistant chat for the eggz Chrome extension is planned for later.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file types can I send?">
    PDF, PNG, JPEG, and WebP — the same formats as [document upload](/help/guides/uploading-documents) in the web app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need an eggz account?">
    No. Extraction and the editable card work as a guest preview. You only sign in (one tap) when you save, which creates your account if you don't already have one.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I capture more than one bill at once?">
    Yes. Share multiple files and review them as a batch; remove any you don't want before saving.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where do my bills end up?">
    In [Expenses](/help/guides/expenses) in the eggz web app, ready to review and publish to [Xero](/help/guides/xero-sync).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I selected Save but it asked me to sign in">
    That's expected the first time — saving needs an eggz account. Sign in (or sign up in one tap) when your assistant prompts you, and your reviewed bills save automatically. Your edits on the card are kept.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The bill card didn't appear">
    Make sure the file actually attached, then ask eggz to capture the bill again. If the card still doesn't show, start a fresh message (or reload the conversation) and re-share the document.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="An amount or category looks wrong">
    Edit it directly on the card before saving — change the supplier, any line, or the **category**, and totals recalculate automatically. AI extraction isn't perfect, so a quick review is always worth it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
