## Expenses overview

The Expenses page brings together two types of outbound transactions:

- **Receipts**: Documents you upload to eggz (photos, PDFs). AI extracts the data automatically.
- **Bills**: Purchase invoices synced from Xero (accounts payable).

Both appear on the Expenses page, separated by tabs.

## Receipts tab

### Viewing receipts

The Receipts tab shows all documents you have uploaded. Each receipt displays:

- **Status**: Pending (processing), Processed (ready to review), or Published (sent to Xero)
- **Merchant name**: Extracted by AI from the document
- **Amount**: Total transaction amount and currency
- **Date**: Transaction date from the receipt
- **Category**: AI-assigned expense category

### Receipt detail page

Click any receipt to open its detail view. Here you can:

- View the original document (PDF viewer or image)
- Review and edit extracted data (merchant, date, amount, line items)
- See the AI extraction summary
- Download the original file
- [Publish to Xero](#publish-a-receipt-to-xero) (as a bill or Spend Money)
- Delete the receipt

### Receipt lifecycle

| Stage | Status | What happens |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Uploaded | Pending | File stored, AI processing queued |
| Processed | Processed | Data extracted, ready for review |
| Submitted | Submitted | Sent for approval (if applicable) |
| Approved | Approved | Approved for payment |
| Published | Published | Created in Xero as Bill or Spend Money |

## Publish a receipt to Xero

Publishing is how a reviewed receipt leaves eggz and becomes a real record in your Xero organisation. Nothing is written to Xero automatically — you review the details first, then approve.

### When the Publish to Xero card appears

The **Publish to Xero** card sits on the receipt detail page, below the extracted data. You'll see it when all of these are true:

- The receipt has finished processing (status **Processed**).
- Your workspace is [connected to Xero](/help/guides/xero-sync). If Xero isn't connected — or the connection has expired — the card shows a **Connect Xero** link to **Settings > Integrations** instead of a publish button.
- Your account has permission to publish to Xero. If you don't see the card at all, ask your workspace admin.

### Bill or Spend Money?

What eggz creates in Xero depends on the receipt's payment status:

| Receipt is | Creates in Xero | Extra input needed |
|------------|-----------------|--------------------|
| **Unpaid** | A **bill** (ACCPAY) you can pay later in Xero | None |
| **Paid** | A **Spend Money** bank transaction | The **bank account** it was paid from — required |

The card tells you upfront which one this receipt will become.

### Review, approve, publish

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Publish to Xero">
    On the receipt's detail page, click **Publish to Xero**. A review dialog opens with the receipt's details pre-filled for your review — you may still need to choose accounts in the next step.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the details">
    Check the contact (taken from the merchant name), the transaction date, the amount, and the line-item count. If something looks wrong, cancel and correct the receipt first — see [the extracted data is wrong](/help/guides/uploading-documents#troubleshooting).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose accounts (if needed)">
    Optionally pick the **expense account** for the line items — leave it blank to use your default expense account. If the receipt is **paid**, you must also choose the **bank account** the money came from; eggz won't let you publish a paid receipt without one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve">
    Click **Publish to Xero** in the dialog. This is the moment eggz writes to Xero — nothing happens without this explicit approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### After publishing

The card changes to **Published to Xero**, showing what was created (a bill or a Spend Money transaction) and when. That's the receipt's final state — the publish button is gone, and each receipt can only be published once, so you can't create duplicates by revisiting the page.

eggz also remembers the link between the receipt and the record it created. When that bill or transaction syncs back from Xero later, eggz recognises it as the same purchase instead of counting it twice — see [How receipts become expenses](/help/guides/receipts-and-bills).

<Note>If Xero rejects the publish — for example, the connection has a problem — eggz shows you the exact error message and the receipt stays unpublished, so you can fix the issue and try again. Nothing is created silently.</Note>

## Bills tab

### Viewing bills

The Bills tab shows purchase invoices synced from Xero. Each bill displays:

- Invoice number and reference
- Contact (supplier) name
- Dates (issued, due)
- Amounts (subtotal, tax, total, paid, due)
- Status (from Xero: Draft, Submitted, Authorised, Paid, Voided)

### Bill detail page

Click any bill to view full details including the PDF attachment (if available in Xero). Bills are read-only -- all editing happens in Xero.

<Note>Bills are synced from Xero and cannot be created, edited, or deleted in eggz. Use Xero to manage your bills, then sync the latest data from **Settings > Integrations**.</Note>

## Customise your table columns

Show the details that matter to you and hide the rest. Every list in eggz — Receipts, Bills, and the tables under **Sales**, **Quotes**, **Customers**, and **Transactions** — lets you pick which columns appear and what order they sit in. Your layout is saved **just for you** on each list, so it's exactly as you left it next time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the column picker">
    Click **Columns** in the table toolbar. A small **"N hidden"** badge shows how many columns are currently tucked away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Show or hide columns">
    Tick a column to show it, or untick to hide it. Search by name if the list is long. A few essential columns are **locked** and always stay visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder columns">
    Use the up and down controls to move a column earlier or later in the table.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll know it worked when the table redraws with your chosen columns — and it stays that way each time you come back.

<Tip>Changed your mind? Click **Reset** in the column picker to snap everything back to the default layout.</Tip>

<Note>Column choices are saved **per person, per list**. Your teammates keep their own layouts, and hiding a column never deletes any data.</Note>

## Uploading new receipts

From the Expenses page, you can upload new documents directly:

- **Drag and drop** PDFs or images onto the upload zone
- **Click** the upload zone to open the file picker
- On mobile, use the **camera button** in the bottom dock

See [Uploading Documents](/help/guides/uploading-documents) for full details on supported formats and processing.
