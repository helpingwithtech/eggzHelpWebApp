## Expenses overview

The Expenses page brings together two types of outbound transactions:

- **Receipts**: Documents you upload to eggz (photos, PDFs). AI extracts the data automatically.
- **Bills**: Purchase invoices synced from Xero (accounts payable).

Both appear on the Expenses page, separated by tabs.

## Receipts tab

### Viewing receipts

The Receipts tab shows all documents you have uploaded. Each receipt displays:

- **Status**: Pending (processing), Processed (ready to review), or Published (sent to Xero)
- **Merchant name**: Extracted by AI from the document
- **Amount**: Total transaction amount and currency
- **Date**: Transaction date from the receipt — or a **Needs date** chip if eggz couldn't find one (see [Receipts without a date](/help/guides/receipt-dates))
- **Category**: AI-assigned expense category

### Receipt detail page

Click any receipt to open its detail view. Here you can:

- See where the receipt sits in its process — the **Process** panel names its stage and the top of the page offers the one next step for it (Resolve, Add a date, or View bill). See [The Process panel](/help/guides/receipts-and-bills#the-process-panel).
- View the original document (PDF viewer or image)
- Check the extracted figures with **GST first** — Total (incl. GST) · GST · Subtotal — and follow the supplier through to its account
- Review the line items and AI extraction summary — amounts are edited on the Receipts list, see below
- Download the original file
- Publish to Xero (as Bill or Spend Money)
- Delete the receipt

### Editing a receipt's amount

On the Receipts list, click a receipt's **Amount** to edit it in place. eggz keeps the total and its line items in agreement automatically — a single-line receipt absorbs the change immediately (with an Undo), while a receipt with two or more lines opens a reconciliation dialog that must balance to $0.00 before it saves. See [Editing totals and line items](/help/guides/editing-amounts) for the full walkthrough.

### Receipt lifecycle

| Stage | Status | What happens |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Uploaded | Pending | File stored, AI processing queued |
| Processed | Processed | Data extracted, ready for review |
| Submitted | Submitted | Sent for approval (if applicable) |
| Approved | Approved | Approved for payment |
| Published | Published | Created in Xero as Bill or Spend Money |

## Bills tab

### Viewing bills

The Bills tab shows purchase invoices synced from Xero. Each bill displays:

- Invoice number and reference
- Contact (supplier) name
- Dates (issued, due)
- Amounts (subtotal, tax, total, paid, due)
- Status (from Xero: Draft, Submitted, Authorised, Paid, Voided)

### Bill detail page

Click any bill to view full details including the PDF attachment (if available in Xero). Bills are read-only -- all editing happens in Xero.

<Note>Bills are synced from Xero and cannot be created, edited, or deleted in eggz. Use Xero to manage your bills, then sync the latest data from **Settings > Integrations**.</Note>

Bills also show a full **Settlement** history — Total, Paid, and Amount due, plus every payment applied — exactly as described for invoices. See [Settlement history](/help/guides/invoices-and-quotes#settlement-history).

## Customise your table columns

Show the details that matter to you and hide the rest. Every list in eggz — Receipts, Bills, and the tables under **Sales**, **Quotes**, **Customers**, and **Transactions** — lets you pick which columns appear and what order they sit in. Your layout is saved **just for you** on each list, so it's exactly as you left it next time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the column picker">
    Click **Columns** in the table toolbar. A small **"N hidden"** badge shows how many columns are currently tucked away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Show or hide columns">
    Tick a column to show it, or untick to hide it. Search by name if the list is long. A few essential columns are **locked** and always stay visible.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder columns">
    Use the up and down controls to move a column earlier or later in the table.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll know it worked when the table redraws with your chosen columns — and it stays that way each time you come back.

<Tip>Changed your mind? Click **Reset** in the column picker to snap everything back to the default layout.</Tip>

<Note>Column choices are saved **per person, per list**. Your teammates keep their own layouts, and hiding a column never deletes any data.</Note>

## Uploading new receipts

From the Expenses page, you can upload new documents directly:

- **Drag and drop** PDFs or images onto the upload zone
- **Click** the upload zone to open the file picker
- On mobile, use the **camera button** in the bottom dock

See [Uploading Documents](/help/guides/uploading-documents) for full details on supported formats and processing.
