## Sales invoices

The **Sales** page shows all invoices synced from your connected Xero account. Invoices represent money owed to you by your customers.

### Invoice list

The invoice table displays:

- **Reference**: Invoice number or reference
- **Customer**: Who the invoice is for
- **Consultant**: Who raised the invoice (if tracked)
- **Status**: Draft, Submitted, Authorised, Paid, or Voided
- **Issue date** and **Due date**
- **Total** and **Balance** (amount remaining)
- **Currency**

You can group invoices by customer, status, or consultant using the table controls.

### Invoice detail

Click an invoice to view the full detail page:

- All fields from the list view, plus notes
- PDF download (if a file is attached)
- Payment date (for paid invoices)
- Edit fields inline (reference, customer, status, dates, amounts)
- Delete the invoice

### Syncing invoices

Click the **Sync** button on the Sales page to pull the latest invoices from Xero.

<Note>Invoices synced from Xero are one-way — changes you make in eggz (edits, deletions) don't sync back to Xero.</Note>

### Publish a draft to Xero

Most invoices arrive by syncing from Xero, but you can also **draft an invoice in eggz** — including one the AI assistant prepares for you — and publish it to Xero as a draft in one click. See [Publish invoices & quotes to Xero](/help/guides/publish-to-xero).

## Quotes

The **Quotes** page shows all quotes synced from Xero. Quotes represent potential future revenue in your sales pipeline.

### Quote list

The quote table displays:

- **Reference**: Quote number
- **Prospect**: The potential customer
- **Owner**: Who manages the quote
- **Status**: Draft, Sent, Accepted, Declined, or Invoiced
- **Amount** and **Currency**
- **Expected close date** and **Valid until date**
- **Win likelihood**: Probability of conversion

You can group quotes by prospect, status, or owner.

### Quote detail

Click a quote to view:

- All list fields plus notes
- PDF download (if attached)
- Linked invoice (if the quote has been converted)
- Delete the quote

### Quotes in analytics

Quotes feed into the **Quote Pipeline** widget on the dashboard, showing the total value of your open quotes for the current month. Click the widget to drill down by month or source type.

<Note>Quotes synced from Xero are one-way — you can't push changes back. But a quote you **draft in eggz** can be published to Xero as a draft. See [Publish invoices & quotes to Xero](/help/guides/publish-to-xero).</Note>
