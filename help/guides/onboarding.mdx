## Welcome to eggz

eggz connects your email to Xero so you can automate invoices, bills, receipts, and more. This guide walks you through your first few minutes -- from your invite to publishing your first document.

<Info>
**eggz is currently invite-only.** We're onboarding founding members personally so we can get every business live in minutes. If you don't have an invite yet, leave your details at [app.eggz.ai/request-invite](https://app.eggz.ai/request-invite) and we'll be in touch. Existing accounts sign in as usual at [app.eggz.ai](https://app.eggz.ai).
</Info>

## Founding members: your first ten minutes

If you received a personal invite, your account and workspace are **already set up** — there's no sign-up form and no setup wizard. Here's what happens:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with your invited email">
    Go to [app.eggz.ai](https://app.eggz.ai) and sign in with the **same email address your invite was sent to**. There's no password to create — use **Continue with Google**, or any of the other sign-in options shown on the screen, with that address and you're in.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first receipt">
    You'll land on a personal welcome from the eggz team. Drop in a receipt — a PDF or a photo — and eggz starts reading it straight away.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Xero">
    While eggz reads your receipt in the background, connect your Xero account. Sign in to Xero, authorise eggz, and pick your organisation if you have more than one.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and publish">
    By the time Xero is connected, your receipt is usually ready — merchant, total, and line items extracted. Open it (it opens in a new tab), check the details, and approve it into Xero. If eggz couldn't read the document, the welcome says so and points you to the receipt so you can review it yourself.

    <Note>**Nothing is written to Xero without your approval.** eggz extracts and prepares your documents, but you always review and approve before anything is published to your books.</Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's the whole loop — capture, read, approve, publish. From there, send eggz the rest of your paperwork with drag-and-drop or the [Chrome extension](/help/unscrambled-tabs/index).

<Tip>Need to get to your dashboard before you've finished? Use the **Skip setup for now** link on any step — you can upload receipts and connect Xero anytime from **Settings > Integrations**.</Tip>

After the welcome, your dashboard shows a short **setup checklist** — install the Chrome extension, get your first 5 documents in, and publish 3 to Xero. Each item ticks itself off as you go, and the whole card disappears once you're done (or dismiss it anytime; it won't nag).

## Prerequisites

- A Xero account (free trial works)
- A Gmail account (for email-to-document automation)
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge)

## Sign up and create your workspace

<Note>While eggz is invite-only, public self-serve sign-up is paused — the **Sign Up** button takes you to the [invite request form](https://app.eggz.ai/request-invite) instead. The steps below describe the self-serve flow for when it reopens.</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.eggz.ai](https://app.eggz.ai) and click **Sign Up**. eggz uses secure authentication powered by WorkOS -- you can sign up with your email or an existing Google account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your workspace">
    After signing in for the first time, you will be asked to create a workspace. eggz pre-fills your workspace name and company domain from your email address.

    - **Workspace name**: Your company or team name
    - **Company domain**: Extracted from your email (e.g. `acme.com`)

    Click **Create workspace** to continue.

    <Tip>Each workspace is a separate organisation. If you work with multiple businesses, you can create a workspace for each one.</Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Complete the onboarding wizard

After creating your workspace, eggz walks you through a **six-step** setup wizard. Most steps are optional; you can finish on the free tier and return later from **Settings > General** or **Settings > Integrations**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your region" icon="globe">
    Select your region to set the correct currency, date format, and tax settings.

    | Region | Currency | Date format | Tax (typical) |
    |--------|----------|-------------|---------------|
    | Australia | AUD | DD/MM/YYYY | GST (10%) |
    | Canada | CAD | YYYY-MM-DD | GST (varies by province) |
    | United States | USD | MM/DD/YYYY | Sales tax (varies) |
    | United Kingdom | GBP | DD/MM/YYYY | VAT (20%) |
    | New Zealand | NZD | DD/MM/YYYY | GST (15%) |

    <Warning>This setting affects how amounts and dates display throughout the app. Choose carefully — you can change region-related defaults later in **Settings > General**.</Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your industry" icon="building">
    Choose the industry that best describes your business. This helps eggz categorise your expenses more accurately.

    Options include trades, professional services, IT and technology, healthcare, retail, hospitality, creative and media, education, transport and logistics, real estate, and other.

    <Tip>If none of the options fit, select **Other** and type your industry. This improves the AI's accuracy when extracting data from your receipts.</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tell us what matters to you" icon="heart">
    eggz tracks the time you save by automating bookkeeping. Choose what you would do with that extra time -- family, more productive work, health, travel, or something else.

    This step is optional and does not affect functionality.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your hourly rate" icon="dollar-sign">
    Enter your hourly rate so eggz can calculate the dollar value of time saved. For example, if you charge $150/hour and eggz saves you 2 hours per week, your dashboard will show $300/week in time savings.

    This step is optional. You can update it later in **Settings > General**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your accounts" icon="plug">
    This is the most important step. Connect your Xero accounting software and Gmail to unlock automation.

    **Connect Xero:**
    1. Click **Connect to Xero**
    2. Sign in to your Xero account when prompted
    3. Authorise eggz to access your Xero data
    4. Select the Xero organisation to connect (if you have multiple)

    **Connect Gmail:**
    1. Click **Connect Gmail**
    2. Authorise eggz to read your emails
    3. eggz will automatically detect invoices, bills, and receipts in your inbox

    <Note>You can skip this step and connect later from **Settings > Integrations**. However, most eggz features require a Xero connection to work.</Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a plan (optional)" icon="credit-card">
    The final step lets you pick a paid plan (Solo, Growth, or Scale) or **Continue on Free**.

    - **Continue on Free** — finish on the free tier with a **one-time allowance of 50 document processing credits** (they do not refill monthly). Upgrade or buy top-ups when you need more.
    - **Paid plan** — you may be redirected to secure checkout; when you return, onboarding completes automatically.

    You can change or upgrade your plan anytime from **Settings** or the in-app pricing area.

    <Tip>This step is optional. You do not need a paid plan to reach the dashboard.</Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Every plan — including Free — **includes the AI assistant** in the web app and the [Chrome extension](/help/unscrambled-tabs/index); plans differ on document allowances, team size, and branding.

After completing the wizard (including **Continue on Free** on the plan step if you skip paid plans), click **Go to dashboard** to start using eggz.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't connect to Xero">
    Make sure you are signed in to the correct Xero account. If you have multiple Xero organisations, you will be asked to choose which one to connect. If the connection fails, try disconnecting and reconnecting from **Settings > Integrations**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I skipped the wizard -- how do I go back?">
    You cannot re-open the wizard, but all the same settings are available in **Settings > General** (region, industry, hourly rate) and **Settings > Integrations** (Xero, Gmail).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My workspace name is wrong">
    Go to **Settings > General** to update your workspace name, timezone, reporting currency, and industry.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
