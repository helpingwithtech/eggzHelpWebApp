## Make the dashboard yours

Your dashboard layout is personal to you. Rearrange it however you like — your changes don't affect anyone else on your team.

<Tip>Out of the box, your dashboard opens with **Revenue**, **Monthly Spend**, **Expense Breakdown**, and **Quote Pipeline**, left to right.</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter customize mode">
    Click the **pencil** (Customize) on the dashboard to start editing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Rearrange widgets">
    **Drag** any widget to move it to a new position.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Resize widgets">
    Grab a widget's **corner** and drag to make it larger or smaller. Widgets snap to clean steps — **quarter, third, half, or full** width, and **one or two units** tall — so the dashboard always tiles neatly, whatever size you choose.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add or remove widgets">
    Add the widgets you want on screen, and remove the ones you don't.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to keep your new layout. Changed your mind? **Cancel**, or press **Esc**, to discard everything and go back to how it was.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>Nothing is saved until you click **Save** — including **Reset to default**, which only takes effect once you save it. If a save fails, you'll see an error and stay in edit mode so you don't lose your changes.</Note>

<Tip>Want to start over? Use **Reset to default** and then **Save** to bring the dashboard back to its original layout.</Tip>

<Warning>While you're customizing, widgets are for dragging and resizing only — charts and links inside them aren't clickable. Click **Save** or **Cancel** first, then interact with a widget as usual.</Warning>
