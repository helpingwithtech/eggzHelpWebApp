## Two AI modes

The AI tab has two assistants. Signed out, you get the **Tabs AI** (the original local assistant). Signed in to your [eggz account](/help/unscrambled-tabs/eggz-account), **both** are available and you can switch between them from the panel header — signing in adds the CRM assistant without taking anything away:

| Mode | Available | What it can do |
|------|-----------|----------------|
| **eggz CRM** | Signed in | Chats with your organization's CRM agent — bills, invoices, quotes, accounts, receipts — streamed from eggz, plus document upload. **No API key needed**; usage is covered by your eggz organization. |
| **Tabs AI (Local AI)** | Always | The original assistant using **your own Anthropic API key** — chat about tabs, **Organise** tab groups, save resources, add tab/page/screenshot sources. |

Switching: in eggz CRM mode, use the **Tabs AI** button in the chat header; in Tabs AI mode, the badge reads **Local AI · switch to eggz CRM**. Your choice is remembered for the browser session.

### eggz CRM mode (signed in)

When you connect your [eggz account](/help/unscrambled-tabs/eggz-account), the AI tab gains a chat with your organization's CRM agent:

- Ask about your **accounts, contacts, bills, invoices, quotes, and receipts** — answers stream in with markdown formatting, and you'll see a small pill when the agent uses a tool (for example reading a document).
- The agent can use your **active browser tab** as context. On an eggz account or contact page, "summarise this account" knows which one you mean; on other sites, the tab title and URL are available as background context.
- **Upload from chat** — the upload buttons sit with the chat, so you can send a document and then ask about it ("what's the status of the bill I just uploaded?").
- **New chat** starts a fresh thread; closing and reopening the panel restores your current one.

No Anthropic API key is required in this mode — usage is covered by your eggz organization.

## Bring your own API key

The extension’s **AI features** use **Anthropic Claude** (model configured in the product). You paste an **Anthropic API key** in **Settings**. The key is stored in **Chrome local storage** for the extension and is used from the **background service worker** — not from random web pages — so your key does not leak into site JavaScript.

<Warning>
Never share your API key or commit it to a repo. Rotate the key in the [Anthropic console](https://console.anthropic.com/settings/keys) if you suspect exposure.
</Warning>

## What the AI can do

Typical capabilities (exact labels may vary by version):

- **Chat** about your tabs or selected context.
- **Organise** tabs into logical **tab groups** within the current workspace.
- **Save resources** — suggestions for folders of saved links derived from open tabs.

The AI only receives what you (or the feature) explicitly sends — for example tab titles, URLs, or extracted snippet content — according to the action you run.

## Search (⌘K / Ctrl+Shift+K)

**Quick search** opens from the keyboard from **any tab** (command is registered in the extension manifest):

- **Mac:** ⌘K  
- **Windows / Linux:** Ctrl+Shift+K  

Use it to find workspaces, tabs, or in-product actions without leaving the keyboard.

## AI assistant shortcut

- **Mac:** ⌘J  
- **Windows / Linux:** Ctrl+Shift+J  

Opens the AI surface from the side panel flow (exact UX depends on your build).

## If you do not use AI

You can leave the API key empty. **Workspaces**, **Performance modes**, **snapshots**, and **backup** work without AI. Only AI-specific actions will prompt you to add a key.
