## What eggz does

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Receipt scanning** | Upload PDFs or photos. AI reads the merchant, date, amounts, and line items. |
| **Xero sync** | Pull invoices, bills, quotes, and customers from Xero into eggz. |
| **Publish to Xero** | Push processed receipts back to Xero as Bills or Spend Money transactions. |
| **Sales pipeline** | Track quotes and invoices synced from Xero with analytics. |
| **Expense analytics** | See monthly spend by category, merchant, and time period. |
| **Gmail integration** | Connect Gmail to detect invoices and receipts in your inbox. |
| **Mobile camera** | Snap a photo of a receipt from your phone and it uploads instantly. |
