## What it does

**Session Time-Travel** takes **automatic snapshots** of your current browsing context (for the focused normal window’s tab set, as implemented in your version) on a **schedule** — for example every few minutes while enabled.

Snapshots let you **rewind** to a recent tab layout if something changed accidentally or you want an older arrangement back.

## Enabling and limits

- Toggle **Session Time-Travel** in **Settings** under smart features.
- The product keeps a **limited ring buffer** of snapshots (for example the most recent 50) so storage stays bounded.

<Note>
Snapshot frequency and retention are implementation details — check your in-product Settings copy for the exact interval.
</Note>

## Restoring

From **Settings**, choose a snapshot and **Restore**. The extension applies the saved workspace and tab metadata. If something fails, try an older snapshot.

<Tip>
Snapshots are **local** to your Chrome profile and extension storage. They are **not** cloud-synced to eggz unless a future integration explicitly adds that.
</Tip>

## Privacy

Snapshots contain **URLs and titles** (and any tab metadata the extension stores). Treat exported backups separately if you need to move machines — see [Backup & import](/unscrambled-tabs/backup-import).
